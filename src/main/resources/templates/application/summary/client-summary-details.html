Summary of client information

To amend your client details click on the edit link relevant to the section you wish to change (for example, to edit 'Basic Details', click the 'Edit Basic Details link'. You will then be taken to the appropriate amendment page. Once changed, please confirm your changes, after which you will return to this page. Once happy with all your changes, click 'Submit Amendments'. Your changes will not be registered in the system until you have done this.
